<h1>About Us:</h1>
<p>The <a href="https://www.google.com/maps?cid=15129672689524195283">ophthalmologists</a> and retina specialists at Vitreous Retina Macula Consultants of New York are internationally known leaders in diagnosing and treating retinal diseases. &nbsp;</p>
<p>VRMNY retina doctors have trained and taught at some of the world's finest and most respected academic institutions. The physicians who comprise the <a href="https://www.vrmny.com/best-retina-specialists-nyc-top-ophthalmologists/">best retina specialists in New York</a> are experts in vitrectomy surgery and hold academic appointments at Lenox Hill Hospital, New York University, and the New York Eye and Ear Infirmary.</p>
<p>Our ophthalmologists in New York City specialize in treating all medical and surgical retinal conditions, emphasizing macular degeneration and diabetic retinopathy, the two leading causes of retinal vision loss.</p>
<p>With state-of-the-art New York ophthalmology centers in Manhattan, VRMNY is the largest <a href="https://www.vrmny.com/">retina vitreous center</a> in the New York Metropolitan area.</p>
<p>Our New York ophthalmologists and eye doctors publish more in the foremost peer-reviewed journals than any other private or academic group in the United States. The practice devotes its time and resources to research and developing new diagnostic and therapeutic strategies. &nbsp;Many current concepts in retinal disease and treatment recognized worldwide were invented at VRMNY.</p>
<p>The attention, empathetic concern, and careful approach of the eye care specialists and their support team of retina surgeons explain the success of VRMNY. &nbsp;The over 60 member staff provides a full range of educational, medical, and surgical services. &nbsp;With an emphasis on patient education, the staff delivers compassionate and empowering care.</p>
